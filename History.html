    HISTORY OF GBHS ATIELA

    GBHS Atiela is a Government Secondary School which was born in 1992 on a hill called Atiela. The school was first of all known as GSS Atiela (Government Secondary School Atiela) which later on became GHS Atiela (Government High School Atiela) and is presently called GBHS Atiela (Government Bilingual High School Atiela).
    In the beginning, it consisted only of the first cycle, Anglophone Section (GSS) which was changed to GHS when the second cycle came in. It remained as such until when the Francophone Section was added making the schoolpurely bilingual (GBHS).
    From then up till date, the school has been led by an able staff, organised, coordinated and aimed at offering the best of education to her students
